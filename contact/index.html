FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to provide water or power?

We are a fully mobile unit. However, having access to a water spigot or power outlet is helpful! If you are at an apartment complex or office without access, please let us know in the notes so we can prepare accordingly.

How long does a detail take?

It depends on the package! A standard Refresh takes about 1-1.5 hours. A Full Proof Detail can take 2-4 hours depending on the condition of the interior.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept Cash, Card, Zelle, and Apple Pay. Payment is due upon completion of the job and your satisfaction.
